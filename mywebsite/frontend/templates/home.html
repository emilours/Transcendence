Welcome!

Pusheen, the adorable and playful cat loved by millions, takes center stage in this project, blending her charm with the world of retro gaming. Inspired by the simplicity and nostalgia of classic arcade games like Pong and Space Invaders, we’ve reimagined these timeless experiences through the lens of Pusheen’s quirky universe.

Originally released in 1972, Pong was a groundbreaking game that helped shape the video game industry. Likewise, Space Invaders in the late 1970s captured imaginations with its alien-invasion theme. Both games became icons of early gaming.

As part of Ecole 42's ft_transcendence project, we've brought these classics to life in a fun and modern way, combining them with Pusheen’s delightful world. Get ready to experience a blend of nostalgia and cuteness as Pusheen takes on the arcade!
